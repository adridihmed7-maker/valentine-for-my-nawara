My Dearest,

i've been thinking about how to start this letter for a while. Because some feelings don't arrive loudly - they grow quietly, little by little, until one day you realize they're already a part of you.

Maybe it was in the way you listened.

Maybe it was in the way you smiled without trying.

Or maybe it was simply how being with you made everything feel calmer, safer, warmer.

You have this gentle way of turning ordinary moments into something special.

Moments I didn't even know I would remember, but somehow, I do. Every single one of them.

This Valentine's Day isn't about perfection or grand promises, it's about presence.

About choosing someone and saying "Here, with you, this is where I want to be."

Words can never truly capture what I feel for you, but I'll try my best...

This Valentine's Day, I want to remind you how deeply you are loved, appreciated, and cherished. You are my everything.

If we're blessed with a daughter one day, I hope she has your smile, your grace, your heart.
